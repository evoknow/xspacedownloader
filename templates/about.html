About XSpace Downloader

XSpace Downloader is a powerful, open-source tool designed to preserve and enhance X (formerly Twitter) Spaces content.

In the age of ephemeral digital content, important conversations and knowledge shared in X Spaces often disappear after 30 days. XSpace Downloader solves this problem by allowing users to download, archive, transcribe, and translate these valuable audio conversations.

Our Mission

To democratize access to information by preserving important conversations and making them accessible across languages and formats. We believe knowledge shared in Spaces should be discoverable, searchable, and permanent.

Our Values
  • Openness: Free and open-source software
  • Privacy: Your data belongs to you
  • Accessibility: Breaking language barriers
  • Preservation: Protecting digital heritage
Key Features
Easy Downloads

Simply paste a Space URL and download. No complex setup or configuration required.

AI Transcription

Convert audio to searchable text with high accuracy using advanced AI models.

Multi-Language Support

Translate content to over 100 languages while preserving context and meaning.

Rich Notes

Add personal notes, timestamps, and even embed videos to create comprehensive records.

Smart Search

Find spaces quickly with powerful search and filtering capabilities.

Privacy First

No passwords stored. Email magic links for secure, hassle-free authentication.
